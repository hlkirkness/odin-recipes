Matcha Latte

Matcha Lattes are delicious drinks that are trendy and easy to make at home

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons matcha powder
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • Ice (optional)
  1. Sift matcha powder into a cup
  2. Add hot water and whisk until clumps are gone
  3. Stir in honey
  4. Add Milk
  5. (optional) Pour over ice
