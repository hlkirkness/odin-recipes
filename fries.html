French Fries

French Fries are made from potatoes that are fried in oil and topped with salt

  • 2 pounds Russet Potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
  • Kosher Salt
  • 2 quarts peanut oil
  1. Put potatoes and vinegar into a saucepan
  2. Add water and boil for 10 minutes
  3. Drain potatoes and allow to dry for 5 minutes
  4. Heat oil
  5. Cook potatoes in oil
  6. Allow potatoes to cool
  7. Fry potatoes again
